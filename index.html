Farmers Friends!

Weather Web Application

In today's economic climate, farmers are faced with an increasing need to manage their resources efficiently and effectively. The goal is to, in simple terms, produce the largest amount of high-quality product, with as few a number of resources as possible. Through a regional analysis of climate across the UK, this application aims to aid farmers in establishing top farming locations to grow various crops. Specifically, Farmers Friends looks at the UK's rainfall and atmospheric temperature data over the years.
